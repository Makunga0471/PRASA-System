# Domain Model

## Domain Model Table

| Entity       | Attributes                                                                 | Methods                                                                 | Relationships                                                                 |
|--------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Train        | trainId, status, speed, currentLocation                                   | start(), stop(), updateLocation(), reportStatus()                      | Runs on Track, monitored by Sensor, serviced by Maintenance                  |
| Track        | trackId, condition, location, status                                      | updateCondition(), reportFault()                                       | Assigned to Train, contains Sensors, repaired by Maintenance                 |
| Sensor       | sensorId, type, status, lastReading, installationDate                     | detectAnomaly(), sendData(), calibrate()                               | Installed on Track, monitors Train & Track, generates Alerts                 |
| Alert        | alertId, message, severityLevel, timestamp, status                        | triggerAlert(), escalate(), notifyOperator(), logEvent()               | Generated by Sensor, handled by Operator, triggers Maintenance               |
| Operator     | operatorId, name, role, shiftTime                                         | monitorSystem(), respondToAlert(), escalateIssue(), logResponse()      | Manages Alerts, coordinates Maintenance                                      |
| Maintenance  | maintenanceId, type, scheduleDate, status, priorityLevel                  | scheduleRepair(), performRepair(), updateStatus(), assignTask()        | Triggered by Alert, repairs Track, services Train                            |

## Business Rules

- Train cannot operate if Track is faulty
- Sensors must be active to generate alerts
- Alerts classified as Low, Medium, High, Critical
- Critical alerts must be escalated immediately
- Maintenance triggered by High or Critical alerts
- All entities must have unique IDs
