# Kanban Board Explanation

## Definition
A Kanban board is a visual project management tool used to track tasks as they move through different stages of a workflow.

## Workflow Visualization
The board uses columns such as To Do, In Progress, Testing, Blocked, and Done to represent the progress of tasks. This makes it easy to see what work is pending, ongoing, or completed.

## Work-In-Progress (WIP)
The Kanban board limits the number of tasks that can be in progress at a given time. This helps prevent team members from being overloaded and ensures tasks are completed efficiently.

## Agile Principles
The Kanban board supports Agile principles by enabling continuous delivery, flexibility, and quick adaptation to changes. Tasks can be updated and moved across stages in real time, improving collaboration and productivity.
